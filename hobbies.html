My Hobbies

This is where I'm passionate about on my free time

Badminton isn’t just something I play it’s something I truly love with all my heart. The moment I step into the court, everything else fades away, and it feels like it’s just me, the racket, and the rhythm of the game. Every hit carries my emotions, my joy, my determination, even my struggles and somehow, badminton understands me in a way nothing else does. I love the sound of the shuttle being struck, the rush of chasing it, and the feeling of giving my all in every rally. It’s not always easy, but that’s what makes me love it even more. Badminton is where I feel alive, where I grow stronger, and where I find a piece of myself every single time I play.

Riding a motorcycle feels like touching true freedom in its purest form. The moment the engine roars to life and the wind brushes against my skin, it’s like all my worries are left behind, fading into the distance with every mile. There’s something deeply emotional about being on the open road no limits, no pressure, just me and the ride. Every turn, every stretch of highway, feels like a quiet escape where I can breathe and simply exist. I don’t just enjoy the ride I feel it in my soul, in the way my heart beats a little calmer and a little braver at the same time. In those moments, I’m not lost I’m free, and that feeling is something I never want to let go of.

Making handmade flowers feels like setting my heart free in the most gentle and beautiful way. With every petal I shape and every detail I carefully create, I pour a piece of myself into something that didn’t exist before. It’s more than just crafting it’s a quiet escape where my thoughts slow down and my emotions flow through my hands. I find joy in watching simple materials turn into something meaningful, something alive with feeling. In those moments, I’m no rushed or pressured. I’m free, fully present, and connected to what I’m creating. Each flower becomes a reflection of my patience, my love, and my creativity, reminding me that even the smallest things I make can hold so much beauty and meaning.

Playing online games feels like finding a place where my heart can finally breathe. The moment I enter that world, it’s as if the weight I carry quietly disappears, replaced by a sense of freedom I can’t always find in real life. Every quest, every battle, every small victory feels deeply personal like I’m not just playing, but living through something meaningful. I get lost in the colors, the stories, and the connections, and for a while, nothing else matters. It’s where I can escape, but also where I can understand myself better, where my emotions have space to exist without judgment. In those moments, I’m not just holding a controller or staring at a screen. I’m feeling everything, and somehow, in that digital world, I feel more real, more alive, and more free than ever.
